Customer Service FAQ
Q: How do I track my order?
A: Enter your order number and email on our 'Track Order' page, or check the tracking link in your confirmation email.
Q: What is your return policy timeframe?
A: Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery in original condition with tags attached for a full refund.
Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, express shipping 1-2 business days, and overnight delivery by next business day.
Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?
A: Orders can be modified within 2 hours of placement. Contact customer service immediately or use the 'Edit Order' option in your account.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and buy-now-pay-later options through Klarna and Afterpay.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Log into your account, go to 'Subscriptions', select the subscription you want to cancel, and click 'Cancel Subscription'.
Q: What if I receive a damaged item?
A: Contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund including return shipping.
Q: Can I change my delivery address?
A: Address changes are possible until your order enters the shipping phase. Check your order status and contact support if still processing.
Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click 'Forgot Password' on the login screen, enter your registered email, and follow the link we send.
Q: What is the expected resolution time for a complaint?
A: We aim to resolve all complaints within 72 hours. You will receive updates via email throughout the process.
Q: Can I update my complaint details after submission?
A: Yes, reply to the confirmation email with additional information or call our support line with your case number.
Q: Do you offer price matching?
A: Yes, we match prices from authorized retailers within 7 days of purchase. Contact customer service with proof of the lower price.
Q: How do I apply a discount code?
A: Enter your code in the 'Promo Code' box during checkout before completing payment. Codes cannot be applied after order placement.
Q: What if an item is out of stock after I order?
A: We'll notify you within 24 hours and offer a full refund, store credit, or the option to wait for restock with an estimated date.
Q: Can I buy now and pay later?
A: Yes, we offer installment payments through Klarna and Afterpay. Select these options at checkout to see available payment plans.
Q: How do I contact customer service?
A: Email support@ourstore.com, call 1-800-HELP-NOW (1-800-435-7669), or use live chat on our website 24/7.
